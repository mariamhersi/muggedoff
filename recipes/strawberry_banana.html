Strawberry Banana Baked Oatmeal

Ingredients:

1/2 Cup Old Fashioned Oats
1 Teaspoon White Sugar
1/4 Teaspoon Baking Powder
Dash of Salt
1/2 Medium Banana
1 Egg
3 Tablespoons Milk (any kind)
1/4 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract
3 Medium Strawberries

Steps:


1. Coat a large, microwavable mug or small bowl with cooking spray.

2. Add oats, sugar, baking powder, and salt to mug. Stir to combine.

3. Add banana, egg, milk, and vanilla extract to oats. Stir until mixed in.

4. Gently fold in strawberries.

5. Microwave on medium power for 3-3 1/2 minutes, or until centre is mostly set. Let stand in microwave for one minute.
