Cheese and Broccoli Egg Whites in a Mug

Ingredients:

1/3 cup Frozen baby broccoli florets
1/2 Cup egg whites
2 Tbsp Finely chopped smoked deli ham
2 Tbsp Cheese of your choice
1 teaspoon Salt and pepper

Steps:

1. Spray inside of a large microwave-safe mug with non-stick cooking spray.

2. Place broccoli in mug; microwave on HIGH 30 seconds.

3. Cut broccoli into smaller pieces.

4. Stir in eggs, 1 tablespoon cheese, ham, salt, and pepper with a fork.

5. Microwave on HIGH 1 minute. Stir; microwave 30 seconds longer or until eggs are set.

6. Sprinkle with remaining 1 tablespoon cheese.
