Omelette (thats right you guessed it, in a mug)

Ingredients:

Large egg
2 Large egg whites
2 tbs Unsweetened almond milk
Pinch of Salt & Pepper
2 tbs tomato, diced (or salsa)
2 tbs of a cheese of your choice
2 tbs of lean turkey, ham or bacon (optional)
2 tbs Red bell pepper, diced
5 Spinach leaves, chopped
2 tsp Chives, chopped
2 tbs Vegetable of choice

Steps:

1. Spray a microwave safe mug with cooking spray. Beat together eggs, milk, seasoning, tomatoes and cheese in the mug.

2. Microwave, uncovered, on high for 1 minute.

3. Remove mug and stir.

4. Cook for an additional 1 minute, or until eggs are completely set.

5. Remove from microwave and top with a sprinkle of additional cheese if desired.

6. Let sit for a few minutes before enjoying or take with you on the go!
